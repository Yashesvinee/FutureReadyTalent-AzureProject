    • MUNNAR TEA GARDENS
    Lying at 1600 meters above sea level in the Western Ghats, Munnar is one of the most sought-after Kerala places to visit and is aptly called the ‘Kashmir of South India’.
    THEKADDY
    Thekkady is one of the best forest tourist places in Kerala and home to wildlife like elephants, bison, deer, Nilgiri langurs and tigers. Cruise along the length of the lake in a tourist boat while sighting animals, birds and reptiles
    ATHIRAPALLY WATERFALLS
    Athirapppily Falls located 63 km from Thrissur district is one of the most visited tourist places in Kerala.The 80 ft waterfalls is located on the Chalakudy River near the Sholayar range.
    VARKALA BEACH
    Flaunting its gracious beauty and charm while having a tryst with the Arabian Sea, Varkala is surely one of the gorgeous and best places for Kerala tourism. It is the only place in Kerala where you can find enticing cliffs by the sea.
    KOLLAM
    Known as the Cashew Capital of the World, Kollam famous for Ashtamudi lake. Kollamis a seaport and a backwater towmn famous for the Jatayu statue.
    TRIVANDRUM
    Called the ‘Evergreen City of India’, Trivandrum is one of the Kerala tourist places that offers an appealing blend of heritage and colonial legacy.
    PALAKKAD
    This ‘Gateway of Kerala’ is a great addition to your itinerary because of its greenery, culture and slow-paced life.
    NILAMBUR
    Enriched with raw nature, a unique blend of culture, exquisite art forms, and shadows of colonial rule, Nilambur is the best place to visit in Kerala. It is famour for it's scenic Railway lines and Teak Forests.
    GAVI
    A leading ecotourism spot in India. The forest houses mammals, birds, butterflies, reptiles and exotic trees. Trek down the path with a guide or take a jungle safari
